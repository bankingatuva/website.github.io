Mission

Three things we actually do

Not a general finance club. Every meeting, every resource, and every relationship we build serves one goal: getting members recruiting-ready for investment banking.

01 · Training

Technical & behavioral prep

Weekly sessions covering the accounting, valuation, and modeling questions that come up in real interviews, plus the behavioral prep most students skip.

02 · Access

Alumni speakers

Direct sessions with UVA alumni working in investment banking, real perspective from people who've been through recruiting recently.

03 · Mentorship

Paired guidance

Every member gets matched with an alumnus or upperclassman for ongoing support through the recruiting process, not just a single session.
